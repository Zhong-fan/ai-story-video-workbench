## Design Context

### Users
Long-form fiction, short-drama, and video creators who need to move story material through scripts, visual assets, storyboards, quality review, and final video production in one project workspace.

### Brand Personality
Creative, capable, and gently cinematic.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use Toonflow as the primary reference for information architecture, dense production-canvas layouts, and workflow interactions. Preserve ChenFlow's pink palette and transparent frosted-glass surfaces instead of copying Toonflow's black-and-white visual theme.

### Design Principles
- Keep the production chain visible and navigable from one project workspace.
- Use pink as a restrained accent, with translucent glass surfaces carrying most of the interface.
- Show real project state, source trace, quality gates, and next actions instead of decorative controls.
- Keep dense creative-production information readable through hierarchy, spacing, and progressive disclosure.
- Rebuild reference interactions in Vue and Pinia without copying Toonflow's technical architecture.
